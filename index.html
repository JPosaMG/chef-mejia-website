About Chef Mejia

With over three decades of culinary experience, Chef Jose Mejia transforms ingredients into memorable dining experiences. From honing his craft at the prestigious Le Basque Catering in Florida to delighting the palates of icons such as Shakira and President Barack Obama, his talent leaves a lasting impression. Now the executive chef of Floratorium Café, Chef Mejia continues to craft exquisite dishes that captivate discerning guests and elevate events of every scale.
